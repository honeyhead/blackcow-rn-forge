# Billing Provider Matrix

## RevenueCat

- pros: entitlement abstraction, analytics integration, cross-store simplification
- cons: extra dependency and vendor layer

## Direct StoreKit / Play Billing

- pros: full control, fewer vendors
- cons: higher implementation and ops cost

## Must-check flows

- purchase success
- purchase pending
- restore purchase
- entitlement mismatch
- trial end
- cancellation and grace period
